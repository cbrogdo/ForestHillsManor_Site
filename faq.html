FAQ

Q: How will I know that my application has been accepted?
A: We will give you a phone call and provide information on what is currently available. You will also be given options on living location.


Q: How will I know if my application was rejected?
A: We will call you letting you know that it was rejected and why. Depending on the problem with the application, you may be allowed to resubmit once it is fixed.


Q: How long is a lease normally for?
A: One year, but can be renew before end of lease.


Q: Is it possible to break a lease early?
A: Yes, provided that three months is paid or you sublease to another party.


Q: What is the process to renew a lease?
A: 2 months prior to end of lease notify us that you would like to renew.
